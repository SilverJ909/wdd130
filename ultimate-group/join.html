This is where you can sign up to be a TO (tournament organizer) or simply sign up for any upcoming tournaments. A TO is a person who will set up the tournament's events including tasks like setting up a time and location for the event, making sure they get enough people to bring their systems so that we can end the tournament on time, and then setting up the bracket on start.gg for people to sign up. Once everything is set then they can determine who will fight who using the bracket. You can also go to this link to go to the start.gg for the most recent tournament.
